Recycle Your Old PC Parts And Electronics.

We are a electronics and hardware recycling and waste managment company dedicated to sustainability. Millions of different circuit boards go to waste each year. These pack landfills and leach toxic metals into the soil and water. We aim to be a zero waste facility, and with the help of our corporate partners, we maintain this standard of eco- friendly waste management for the next generation to come.

Our company uses an innovative method to extract all toxic materials from circuit boards and repurpose them for reuse or careful disposal. We can accept any product with a circuit board or wiring. We offer repairs and resale of select products. For more information, follow the links.
